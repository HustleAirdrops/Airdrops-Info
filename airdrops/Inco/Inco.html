Comfy Platform

Visit Comfy Platform

Instructions

  • Select USDC and cUSDC.
  • Enter the token amount and click Mint.

  • Click Shield, select Max, and approve again.
  • To unshield and send:
    • Click Unshield, enter the amount, and approve.
    • Click the send icon, enter your ETH address and token amount, then approve.
